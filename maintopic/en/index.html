Website Information

  "Mizuki's Shorts Museum" is a website dedicated to collecting and popularizing short video culture.

  • Founder: Shimada Mizuki
  • Date of Establishment: 2025-02-19

  Since the author did not have the habit of saving videos in the early days, many media materials were not obtained before they were removed from the shelves (such as videos that Kuaishou has removed from the shelves). If you have relevant materials, please submit them to my email. Submission requirements: The original video link is downloaded or there is no post-production modification (with or without short video platform watermark).
